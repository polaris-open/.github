# Code of Conduct

Polaris should be a respectful, inclusive, and learning-oriented community.

---

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful to everyone, regardless of experience level.
- Assume good intent, but own the impact of your words.
- Disagree with ideas, not people.
- Give context when raising concerns or objections.
- Welcome beginners without lowering the quality of the work.
- Be constructive and specific in feedback.

---

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment in any form.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Spam or repetitive noise.
- Doxxing or sharing private information about others.
- Sharing sensitive, private, or confidential data in issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Intentionally misleading others about technical topics.
- Using the community to promote unsafe, unethical, or irresponsible AI use.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, remove content, close issues or PRs, or block users who violate this code of conduct.
